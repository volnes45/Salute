    The Story of Pulwama Attack

    On **14th February 2019**, a **suicide bomber** attacked a convoy of **CRPF personnel** in **Pulwama, Jammu & Kashmir**. The attack was carried out using a vehicle loaded with **explosives**, crashing into a bus carrying our brave soldiers.

    **40 CRPF soldiers** were martyred in this cowardly act. The attack led to national mourning, and in response, the **Indian Air Force (IAF) conducted the Balakot airstrike** to target terrorist camps in Pakistan.
